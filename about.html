About CodeKitBox

Last updated: June 14, 2026

CodeKitBox provides fast, practical utilities for developers, technical learners, writers, and data workers.

Our approach

Where practical, tools run locally in your browser. This keeps common inputs such as JSON, JWT payloads, text, and timestamps on your device instead of sending them to an application server.

What CodeKitBox offers

The site includes encoding, decoding, formatting, conversion, generation, comparison, and text-processing tools. We aim to keep each tool easy to understand and useful without requiring an account.
