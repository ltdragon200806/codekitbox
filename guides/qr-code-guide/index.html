How to Create a QR Code That Scans Reliably

A good QR code is easy to scan, points to accurate content, and has enough contrast and empty space around it.

Open the free tool

Step-by-step

  1. Enter the complete URL or text.
  2. Generate the QR code.
  3. Test it with more than one phone camera.
  4. Keep sufficient white space around the code.
  5. Retest after resizing or printing.

Important tips

  • Use a complete HTTPS URL.
  • Shorter content creates a simpler, easier-to-scan code.
  • Avoid low contrast, distortion, and very small print sizes.

Frequently asked questions

Do QR codes expire?

Static QR codes do not expire, but the destination URL can stop working.

How small can a QR code be?

It depends on scanning distance and content complexity. Always test the final size.
