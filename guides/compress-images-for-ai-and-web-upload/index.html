How to Compress Images Before Uploading to AI Tools, Forms, or Websites

Many AI tools, forms, and websites limit image size. Compressing or resizing a picture first can make uploads faster and more reliable.

Open the free tool

Step-by-step

  1. Choose the image you want to upload.
  2. If the file is very large, resize it to a practical width such as 1200px or 1600px.
  3. Compress the image as JPEG or WebP.
  4. Check the output file size and preview the visual quality.
  5. Use the compressed image for the AI tool, form, website, or email attachment.

Important tips

  • Keep the original image if it is important.
  • Use PNG for transparency or sharp screenshots, and JPEG or WebP for photos.
  • Do not over-compress images that contain small text or product details.

Frequently asked questions

Will compression affect AI image understanding?

Mild compression usually works well, but very blurry or tiny images may reduce quality for OCR and visual analysis.

What file size should I aim for?

For many uploads, under 1MB or 2MB is convenient, but the right limit depends on the service.

Are images uploaded by this tool?

No. The image compressor processes images locally in your browser.
