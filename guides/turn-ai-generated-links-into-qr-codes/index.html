Turn AI-Generated Links, Event Text, or Instructions into QR Codes

AI is useful for drafting event descriptions, menu text, instructions, and landing page copy. A QR code makes the final link or short text easy to open from posters, screens, and printed materials.

Open the free tool

Step-by-step

  1. Use AI to draft the message, event description, or landing page text.
  2. Edit the text and verify every URL before sharing.
  3. Copy the final HTTPS link or short text.
  4. Generate the QR code and download it.
  5. Scan the QR code with more than one phone before printing or publishing.

Important tips

  • Use a short, stable HTTPS URL when possible.
  • Do not put long paragraphs into a QR code if a landing page would be easier to update.
  • Always test the code at the final printed or displayed size.

Frequently asked questions

Can I put AI-generated text directly into a QR code?

Yes, but short text scans better. For long content, link to a page instead.

Do QR codes expire?

Static QR codes do not expire, but the destination link can stop working.

Should I verify AI-generated URLs?

Yes. Always open and check links before creating a QR code.
